Grand Mercure , Bangalore


Location

  • Located in Koramangala, Bangalore, Grand Mercure offers extended stay and business travel accommodation with kitchens, living areas and private balcony.
  • It has a 24-hour fitness centre, an outdoor pool, a spa and 2 restaurants.

Special Features

  • Each air-conditioned room at the Grand Mercure Bangalore has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, DVD player, music system and work desk.
  • It has a dining table and ironing facilities.
  • Guests can enjoy massages and facial treatments at the Tattva Spa.
  • A well-equipped business centre provides high speed internet access and various office services. Free parking is available.
  • The poolside By The Blue Bar and Restaurant serves Indian dishes.
