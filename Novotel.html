Novotel , Hyderabad


Location

  • Located in the business hub of Hyderabad, HITEC City, this 5-star hotel is connected to Hyderabad International Convention Centre.
  • The hotel is at a 45-minute drive from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.

Special Features

  • The hotel comprises of newly renovated 288 well-appointed rooms & suites.
  • Elegantly decorated with modern-day amenities like satellite TV, a minibar, and tea/coffee making facilities.
  • The rooms offer views of the pool, vast green spaces and city skyline.
  • For great wine and dine options, both indoors or by the poolside, there are 4 dining options – Food Exchange, La Cantina, Le Café and The Bar – offering local and contemporary cuisines.
  • The hotel also offers a myriad of recreational activities: 24-hrs fitness centre, jogging track, swimming pool, gaming zone, spa & salon facilities.

What's Nearby

  • It shares a close proximity to corporates & IT Parks like Cyber Tower (2.2 km), Mindspace (3.9 km), Google (3.7 km), Gachibowli (5 km), IBM (6.5 km), Accenture (8 km) and Microsoft (8 km).
  • People particularly like the location — they rated it 8.6 for a two-person trip.
